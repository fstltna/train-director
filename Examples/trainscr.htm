This scenario shows how to use scripts associated with Trains.

The scripts associated with the 3 trains will print some messages
in the alert window, as each train triggers different events.

Description of the activity

Let T1 enter and let it stop at the first signal.
Then clear the signal and let T1 arrive at S1 and later at S2.
Assign T1 to T2.
Let T2 exit via B. Meanwhile, T3 will enter and arrive at T1.
Reverse T3's direction, and then shunt it out of the territory.

Also, T1's departure from S1 can be randomly delayed by 60 seconds.
