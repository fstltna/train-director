This script is an example on how one can associate
an HTML page to an icon on the track. When the
user clicks on the icon, the script tells Train Director
to show this page in the "Scenario Info" tab.

In this page the author of the scenario can provide
information on the simulation, or even information on
the specific location that the icon represents,
possibly using pictures of the location, as can be
seen here on the right.
