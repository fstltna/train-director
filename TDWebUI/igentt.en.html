This page is used to attempt to automatically generate schedule files for Train Director.

Web pages for stations in the layout must be saved in the "$TDHOME/gentt/stations" directory, where $TDHOME is the location where the traindir executable is.

This page will automatically find the saved pages and attempt to retrieve the schedule for each train listed in those pages.

Supported pages are:

  • Pages from the Swiss Federal Railroads web site on-line departure and arrival.
  • Pages from the stations listed on the Italian's fan web site e656.net.
